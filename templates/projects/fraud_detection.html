Forecasting the Severity of Road Accidents in the USA

Category: Machine Learning | Predictive Analytics

Developed a predictive model to estimate road accident severity based on various factors such as weather, road type, and time of day.

Implemented machine learning models (Decision Tree, Random Forest, K-Nearest Neighbors, and Logistic Regression) to predict severity levels. Evaluated performance using accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score, with Random Forest achieving the highest accuracy of 86.52%. The system provides insights to transportation authorities for proactive safety measures.
